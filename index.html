Inspiration

Since i was a little girl i had seen my father get into the kitchen on his weekoffs and start cooking amazing lip-smacking meals for all of us, I used to be his apperentice. He used to find some kind of keep in brewing something up. After i grew up i always was interested in cooking but due to studies and work it was backlogged.

I got sometime to myself after marriage to experiment with the dishes and cuisines, nothing like an empty kitchen to yourself and someone who appreciates whatever you make. After some initial failures the parental cooking gene in me kicked in and lead to some amazing dishes. Being happily a bit over weight from all the food i can now say that it has become my happy place to like my Dad.
